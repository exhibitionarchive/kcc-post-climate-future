About

SPECULATIVE VISIONS OF A POST-CLIMATE FUTURE explores artistic imaginations of worlds shaped by climate transformation, technological mediation, and speculative futures. Bringing together Korean artists and a Hong Kong local collaborative project, the exhibition presents multimedia works that question how humans, environments, and nonhuman systems may coexist in uncertain futures.

Curatorial Concept

Through moving image, installation, sound, interactive systems, speculative storytelling, and experimental media practices, the exhibition approaches climate futures not as distant scenarios, but as emotional, cultural, and political conditions already emerging in the present.

Theme

Speculative Futures

The exhibition invites audiences to engage with artistic speculation as a method for imagining ecological uncertainty, technological adaptation, and transformed social realities.

Media

Multimedia Practices

Participating works include VR, moving image, sound, robotics, installation, interactive media, and digital storytelling.
