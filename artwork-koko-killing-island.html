Koko Killing Island: Tour d'Epicure

Inhwa Yeom, 2022. PC-based VR, installation, NFT.

Koko Killing Island: Tour d'Epicure is a VR stage where audience-performers become eco-tourists on a virtual island that has survived the climate crisis. Guided by virtual hosts, visitors enter a fictional tourism system that mixes ecological spectacle, social ritual, accessibility, and survival after environmental collapse.

The work places the audience inside an invented post-climate destination, asking how tourism, leisure, desire, and technological mediation might continue when climate crisis has already reshaped the conditions of everyday life.
