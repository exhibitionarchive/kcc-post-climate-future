Perceive (Panorama)

Kohui. Single-channel audiovisual work.

Perceive (Panorama) transforms automobile routes, leaf patterns, stock-chart swings, and ocean tides into a continuous musical panorama. The work treats data and natural structures as rhythmic materials, making familiar systems newly audible.

By converting patterns of movement and order into sound, the installation asks visitors to listen to relationships that are usually read visually or statistically. It turns perception itself into a field where environmental, social, and economic signals overlap.

2026 Korea-China-Japan Year of Cultural Exchanges

2025-2026 中日韓文化交流年

The “2025–2026 Korea-China-Japan Year of Cultural Exchanges” was officially proclaimed at the Trilateral Summit on May 27, 2024, under the shared recognition that culture serves as a bridge connecting the three nations. Jointly organized by the Ministry of Culture, Sports and Tourism of the Republic of Korea, the Ministry of Education, Culture, Sports, Science and Technology of Japan, and the Ministry of Culture and Tourism of the People’s Republic of China, the initiative is designed to deepen cultural exchange and strengthen people-to-people ties among the three countries.
