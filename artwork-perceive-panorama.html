Artwork

Perceive (Panorama)

Kohui. Sound-based installation.

Perceive (Panorama) transforms automobile routes, leaf patterns, stock-chart swings, and ocean tides into a continuous musical panorama. The work treats data and natural structures as rhythmic materials, making familiar systems newly audible.

By converting patterns of movement and order into sound, the installation asks visitors to listen to relationships that are usually read visually or statistically. It turns perception itself into a field where environmental, social, and economic signals overlap.
