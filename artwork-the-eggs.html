Artwork

The Eggs

Sangdon Kim. Painting, sculptures.

The Eggs is Sangdon Kim's updated work for the exhibition. The project reimagines the egg as a symbol of spiritual transformation, collective resilience, and latent life, connecting ritual, social memory, and cosmic imagery.

The work brings together paintings and sculptural forms to consider how fragile beginnings can carry histories of protection, renewal, and change. Within the exhibition's post-climate frame, the egg becomes an image of survival and uncertain futurity.
