SPECULATIVE VISIONS OF A POST-CLIMATE FUTURE

This exhibition gathers multimedia artworks that imagine possible worlds after climate transformation. Through speculative narratives, moving images, installation, sound, and embodied encounters, the project asks how artistic imagination can help us sense futures that are already arriving.
