SPECULATIVE VISIONS OF A POST-CLIMATE FUTURE 氣候未來式

To engage the Hong Kong art and cultural community, Hong Kong Korean Cultural Center collaborated with the Studio For Narrative Spaces to curate the exhibition, bringing together five Korean artists and a Hong Kong collaborative art project that use speculative narratives and technological experimentation to reimagine possible post-climate futures through VR, sound, robotics, games, and multimedia installations.
