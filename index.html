SPECULATIVE VISIONS OF A POST-CLIMATE FUTURE

A 2026 open call exhibition at the Korean Cultural Center in Hong Kong, curated by Studio for Narrative Spaces. The exhibition brings together Korean artists, creative studios, and a Hong Kong collaborative art project to imagine post-climate futures through VR, sound, robotics, games, AI-generated moving image, and multimedia installation.
